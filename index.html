Drink Mirinda

Mirinda is a brand of soft drink originally created in Spain in 1959 and now owned by PepsiCo with global distribution.
It is available in fruit varieties including orange, citrus, grapefruit, apple, strawberry, raspberry, pineapple, pomegranate, banana, passionfruit, lemon, hibiscus, vanilla, guarana, tangerine, pear, watermelon and grape flavours as well as tamarind.
